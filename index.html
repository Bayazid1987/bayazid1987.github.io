About Us | আমাদের সম্পর্কে

Based in the heart of Dhaka city at Purana Paltan, Modhumoti Trade Link operates with a mission to deliver safe and fresh agricultural products to international markets. We maintain strong compliance with export standards and provide excellent customer service.
